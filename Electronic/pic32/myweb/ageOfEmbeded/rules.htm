For a long time, two neighbouring kingdoms, the NAND dynasty and the NXOR lineage, have been rivals. Today, they have decided to settle their quarrels in a final battle. Each player will take control of one of those kingdoms, and must lead it to victory!

Goal

The goal of this game is to destroy all the castles of the enemy kingdom. To do so you have to move your soldiers on each of the castle of your opponent, they will then destroy them. If you lose all your castles, you have lost the battle!

Moving around

At each turn every one of your soldiers will be able to move 2 tiles before needing a rest (the next turn). If you buy a new soldier you will have to wait the next round in order to move him, the soldier being a fresh recruit. You can also walk across rocks but not stay on them. Soldiers can also destroy a castle or engage a fight with the enemy with their moves.

Buy soldiers

During your turn, you can recruit soldiers. To do so you have to press the buy button in the home page. You can buy as many soldiers as you want but you got to have enough money to pay for them! Money is earned during your opponent's turn, by chasing the blue coins in the field with your square worker.

Fight

When two soldiers of different kingdoms meet, they will start a fight. The soldier that loses the battle will be killed by the winner. There are tree different types of figth that can occur.
Speed Game : destroy the shields

It is a game where 12 enemy shields will appear on you screen. The goal will be to touch all of them in order to destroy the shields. But hurry up; you have to be faster than your opponent if you want to win!

Pattern Game : aim for the target!

It is a game where some bow and arrow practice targets will briefly appear on you screen. The goal will be to redo the pattern without any error : shoot the arrows at the right positions! The goal is again to be faster than your opponent.

Snake game : the battle of the monsters

It is a game where each player control a snake, invoked by the battling soldiers. The first snake to bump into a wall or another snake will lose the battle.
