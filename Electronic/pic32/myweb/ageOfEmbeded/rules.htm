Goal

The goal of this game is to destroy all the enemy's castle. To do so you have to move your soldiers on your enemy's castle.

Shifting

At each round you can move your soldiers from 2 cases. If you buy a new soldier you will have to wait the next round in order to move him. You can also walk trow the rocks but not stay on them.

Buy soldiers

At each round you can buy soldier. To do so you have to press the buy button in the home page. You can buy as many soldiers as you want but you have to have enough money

Fight

Speed Game

is a game where 12 boxes will appear on you screen. The goal will be to touch all of them in a minimum amount of time.

Pattern Game

is a game where a pattern will appear on you screen. The goal will be to redo the pattern without any error in a minimum amount of time

Curve Fever

is a game where each player control a snake. The first one that touch him self, a boarder or the enemy lost the fight
